Welcome

Welcome to Flamingo Landing by QRP Coastal Living. This home is designed to be comfortable, easy, and thoughtfully prepared so guests can settle in quickly and enjoy the Charleston area.

Arrival Basics

  • Review your confirmed reservation messages before arrival.
  • Use the guest-only instructions provided through the booking platform for private access details.
  • Let the host know promptly if anything seems off when you arrive.

House Basics

  • Make yourself comfortable and treat the home with care.
  • Use provided amenities respectfully so the next guest gets the same polished experience.
  • Keep outdoor areas tidy after use.
  • Report maintenance concerns quickly instead of waiting until check-out.

Check-Out Expectations

  • Gather personal items and check drawers, closets, and chargers.
  • Dispose of trash in the proper area.
  • Load or start dishes as directed in the guest-only instructions.
  • Turn off lights and secure the home before leaving.
  • Send the host a message if anything needs attention.
